Lundbreck Falls, AB

Lundbreck Falls is a waterfall located in the Pincher Creek Municipal District in Southwestern Alberta. It is on the Crowsnest River along Highway AB-3A west of the hamlet of Lundbreck, and is located 68km west of Fort MacLeod, 25km northwest of Pincher Creek and 200km south of Calgary. Lundbreck Falls is a part of the Lundbreck Falls Provincial Recreation Area, and contains a viewing area and campgound. The campgound is bookable via Alberta Parks' website.

The image above shows the waterfalls, taken on the rocks on the north side of the water. The rocks along the shore are accessible via a set of stairs located near the Highway 3A bridge.

More Information: Lundbreck Falls PRA - Alberta Parks

Salmon Arm, BC

Salmon Arm is a town in central British Columbia, approximately 100km east of Kamloops, 110km north of Kelowna and 104km west of Revelstoke. It is one of the smaller towns in BC, having a population of around 24,000. Its location provides an impressive backdrop with nearby mountains and the lakeshore. Salmon Arm is also a great location for picnics, especially in the wharf park.

The image above shows the Salmon Arm marina, taken at the end of the wharf facing north. There are washroom facilities and an ice cream shop located at this spot. Boat rentals are also available during peak season.

More Information: Salmon Arm Official Website

Lethbridge, AB

Lethbridge is a city in southeastern Alberta, 106km north of the US Border, 48km east of Fort MacLeod and 213km south of Calgary. It is a larger city with a population of just over 100,000 people. Due to its location in the prairies, it can get relatively hot compared to other cities in the area. Lethbridge Airport hosts an annual airshow each July.

The image above is of the Lethbridge Viaduct, the largest railway structure in Canada. The imaage was taken from the viewing area on the east end of the bridge in Indian Battle Park.

More Information: Lethbridge Official Website
