Locations

Lundbreck Falls, AB

Lundbreck Falls is a waterfall located in the Pincher Creek Municipal District in Southwestern Alberta.

It is on the Crowsnest River along Highway AB-3A west of the hamlet of Lundbreck.

Lundbreck Falls is located 68km west of Fort MacLeod, 25km northwest of Pincher Creek and 200km south of Calgary.

Salmon Arm, BC

Salmon Arm is a town in central British Columbia, approximately 100km east of Kamloops, 110km north of Kelowna and 104km west of Revelstoke.

Lethbridge, AB

Lethbridge is a city in southeastern Alberta, 106km north of the US Border, 48km east of Fort MacLeod and 213km south of Calgary.
